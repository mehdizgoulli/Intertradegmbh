About Intertrade GmbH

A trusted name in the international food trade

Based in Germany, Intertrade GmbH specializes in the sourcing and distribution of fast-moving consumer goods in the food & beverage segment. We connect leading manufacturers with retailers, wholesale groups and importers worldwide, giving our clients direct access to some of the most in-demand international brands.

Our central European location ensures efficient storage and transport, so we can deliver high-quality products reliably and on schedule — from single pallets to full containers.

  • Direct access to leading international brands
  • Wholesale (B2B) supply for businesses
  • Full-pallet and container shipments
  • Compliance with European quality standards
